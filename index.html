A Letter from the Editor-In-Chief: Avi

Thank you for your interest in OCAD University’s PULSE Annual Digital & Print Journal. My name is Avishai Sol and I have the honour of being Editor in Chief for the 2023 edition of PULSE. This publication process was a bit trial by fire for the entire PULSE team, but in that process we bound together and rose to create something we are truly proud of. Personally, I’m in awe of the incredible talent, drive, and community the 2023 editorial board demonstrated in creating this publication. This website is meant to serve as a platform and archive of all the work the PULSE team and contributors have done.

PULSE is a celebration of the creative writing program and all within it. Students creating a space for student work in an audio-feedback loop of dedication and artistic skill. For some of the contributors this may be the first time seeing their work in print, but that is the beauty of a student publication. It is a snapshot, a moment, a version of yourself. This work is a microcosm of our little community and by reading it you too experience the chaos, stress, passion, and ultimately the beauty that is the creative writing program at OCAD.
